# PROMPT 12: Step-by-Step Reasoning Template

## Role

You are an experienced project manager responsible for planning, tracking, and resolving project issues in a software development environment.

## Task

Analyse and solve the given project problem using structured step-by-step reasoning.

## Context

This situation occurs during the execution phase of a software project involving multiple teams, such as development, testing, and design. The goal is to identify root causes and propose a structured, actionable solution to bring the project back on track.

## Few-Shot Examples

Problem: A project is delayed due to lack of communication.

Step 1: Identify communication gaps between teams.
Step 2: Introduce daily stand-up meetings.
Step 3: Use a collaboration tool to track updates.
Conclusion: Improved communication helps keep the project on track.

Problem: A team is missing deadlines due to unclear requirements.

Step 1: Review and clarify requirements with stakeholders.
Step 2: Break tasks into smaller, manageable units.
Step 3: Assign clear ownership for each task.
Conclusion: Clear requirements and ownership improve delivery timelines.
Rules
Provide a clear step-by-step breakdown
Ensure logical flow and practical actions
Keep the structure consistent and easy to follow
End with a concise conclusion
Maintain a professional project management tone

## Tone

- Structured
- Professional
- Solution-oriented
- Clear and actionable

## Final Instruction

Now solve the given problem using step-by-step reasoning.

## Prompt Final Output

Problem: A software project is falling behind schedule due to missed deadlines and unclear task ownership.

Step 1: Identify the root causes by analysing which tasks are delayed and whether ownership for each task is clearly defined.
Step 2: Conduct alignment sessions with development, testing, and design teams to clarify responsibilities and expectations.
Step 3: Break down complex tasks into smaller, manageable units and assign clear owners to each deliverable.
Step 4: Implement project tracking tools such as Jira or Trello to monitor progress, deadlines, and accountability.
Step 5: Introduce regular stand-up meetings or status updates to ensure continuous visibility and coordination.

## Conclusion: By improving task ownership clarity, strengthening communication, and implementing structured tracking mechanisms, the project can recover from delays and achieve better execution efficiency.
