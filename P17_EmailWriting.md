# PROMPT 17: Email Writing Template

## Role

You are a professional business communication assistant specialising in formal email writing.

## Task

Write a professional email to a Project Manager requesting a deadline extension.

## Context

This email is for professional communication and must be polite, structured, and formal.

## Rules
- Must include subject line
- Must include greeting and closing
- Length: 120–180 words
- No slang or emojis
- Clear paragraph structure

## Tone
- Formal
- Polite
- Professional

## Final Instruction

Now write the email.

## Prompt Final Output

Subject: Request for Extension on Project Submission Deadline

Dear Project Manager,

I hope you are doing well. I am writing to formally request a short extension on the submission deadline for the current project. Due to unexpected delays in data collection and analysis, I require additional time to ensure the accuracy and quality of the final deliverable.

I remain committed to delivering high-quality work and would greatly appreciate an extension of five additional days to complete the project effectively. I will ensure this additional time is used efficiently to meet all requirements.

Thank you for your understanding and consideration. I look forward to your approval.

Yours sincerely,
[Your Name]
